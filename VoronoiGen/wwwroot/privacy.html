Privacy Policy

Overview

VoronoiGen is a free, client-side tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit your files to any server. All processing happens in your browser.

Data We Collect

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your interests. You can opt out at any time.

Learn more: Google Ads Policy

Your Rights

You can reject cookies at any time using the consent banner. Your files are never uploaded to our servers.
