About VoronoiGen

VoronoiGen is a free, client‑side Voronoi DXF generator designed for engineering, fabrication, and generative art workflows. It runs entirely in your browser; your files never leave your device.

What makes it different?

  • Clean DXF export for CNC/laser
  • Poisson disk sampling and optional Lloyd relaxation
  • Controls for offsets, gaps, min area, and aspect ratio
